Intro

Your starting class "Mage/Fighter/Rouge" are just routes into the real classes such as Knight, Fire-Mage, Wind-mage, Archer, Mite-spirit. This is where you can discover and become a class of a higher rank. Each player get`s two classes before advancement. Each combination of classes helps decide your advanced class.

Each class level has it`s own stat distributions when you level up. Although these are all the same once you become an advanced class. Points are distributed depending on your highest class type.

Example:

Rouge / Mite-Spirit / Adamantoise-Spirit. Since the highest level class here is rare, then the player gets the stat points of a rare class.

0-50

Normal Class

In this bracket you gain 2 point per level to spend on stats, and 1 point to spend on hobbies. At level 1 all classes get 2 stat points and 1 hobby point. This gives a total of 100 stat points and 50 hobby points at leve 50.

Rare Class

In this bracket you gain 3 point per level to spend on stats, and 1 point to spend on hobbies. At level 1 all classes get 2 stat points and 1 hobby point. This gives a total of 150 stat points and 50 hobby points at leve 50.

Epic Class

In this bracket you gain 3 point per level to spend on stats, and 2 point to spend on hobbies. At level 1 all classes get 2 stat points and 1 hobby point. This gives a total of 150 stat points and 100 hobby points at leve 50.

Legendary Class

In this bracket you gain 4 point per level to spend on stats, and 3 point to spend on hobbies. At level 1 all classes get 2 stat points and 1 hobby point. This gives a total of 200 stat points and 100 hobby points at leve 50.

Growth Class

You gain points depending on what level of growth you have achieved.

Unique Class

The points earned per level are dependent on your class, please see your specfic class for details.

Quest Class

The points earned per level are dependent on your class, please see your specfic class for details.

All Classes earn the following:

  • 0 - Starting skills unlocked
  • 10 - Gain class specific ability
  • 30 - basic skills unlocked(cap raised from 0 to 3)
  • 50 - Class ability advances

Advanced Class

Once you pick an advanced class you remain level 100. These are levels of advanced classes ranging from normal to legendary. (unique/quest classes may not pick an advanced class.) Each advanced class at level 100 has 400 stat points and 200 hobby points.

Class advancement will depend on the choices you made.

Such as your starting class, Class specific advancement(Magic element, Spirit, Job). As well as some of the less obvious choices towards class such as: your race, quest choices and avlibility of mentors.
