Intro

Skill points

Skill points are use to level up skills, you gain one per level. This is true for all classes and mostly true for unique & gold classes.

Stat points

Your starting class "Mage/Fighter/Rouge" are just routes into the real classes such as Knight, Fire-Mage, Wind-mage, Archer, Mite-spirit. This is where you can discover and become a class of a higher rank. Each player get`s two classes before advancement. Each combination of classes helps decide your advanced class.

Each class level has it`s own stat distributions when you level up. Although these are all the same once you become an advanced class. Points are distributed depending on your highest class type.

Example:

Rouge / Mite-Spirit / Adamantoise-Spirit. Since the highest level class here is rare, then the player gets the stat points of a rare class.

Advanced Class

You can change to an advanced class at level 100. There are ranks of the advanced classes ranging from normal to legendary (unique/quest classes may not pick an advanced class). Each advanced class at level 100 has 400 stat points.

Class advancement will depend on the choices you made.

Such as your starting class, Class specific advancement(Magic element, Spirit, Job). As well as some of the less obvious choices towards class such as: your race, quest choices and avlibility of mentors.
