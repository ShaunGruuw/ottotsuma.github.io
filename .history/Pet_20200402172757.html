Personal Summons

sub tittle

Personal summons are unlocked at level 70 for all classes. Each class has a different name for them, related to the way they obtain a personal summon. For a Mage it is a Familia, The Fighter a Compainion, The Rouge a Spirit Projection. The common name for them is Pets.

Mages must go into meditation and project themselves into another realm from which they must capture a pet and bring their souls back though to this realm.
Rouges must deafeat and then tame monsters to absorb their souls, later once the monsters soul can be controlled you can project the soul into reality.
Fighters can tame monsters though combat not killing the animal but forming a bond though the fight.
The combat can be skipped if the monster has a high oppinion of the user.

Pets have abilities and skills that can be shared with their user or kept to themselves. Usually pets that share their abilities & skills with their users proform worse in combat alone then thoes that do not. The rarity of pets does not make them better then more common pets, what makes a good pet (for pure combat) is one that adds or blends with the users combat style.
In simple terms this means although pets like Indrik do not give you stat boots they can make up for a users poor move speed. On the flip side Axoltl`s are very poor in combat but the regeneration they give could be very useful to high HP users.
