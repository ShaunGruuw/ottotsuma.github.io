Crafting

Junior crafter (9), Able to craft Junk level items, Able to craft Basic Level items (With skill level % chance).

Adept Crafter (9), Able to craft Rare Level items (With skill level % chance).

Expert crafter (9), Able to craft Epic Level items (With skill level % chance).

Master crafter (9), Able to craft legendary Level items (With skill level % chance).

Godly crafter (9), Able to craft Growth Level items (With skill level % chance).

In total that gives: Basic: 45%, Rare: 36%, Epic: 27%, Legendary: 18%, Growth 9%. This is the % chance of making the item a level above what you are aiming for. So making an Rare item with Rare parts has a 27% chance of being made as Epic.
