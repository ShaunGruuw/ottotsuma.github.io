Humans

  • Calculator

Humans are a common race in Monad. Each race has something that puts it apart from the rest. Humans are the default race in Monad for every player, which makes it easy to take some of the human perks for granted.

Humans inhabit almost every part of the world of Monad where in the majority of those places they make up the majority of the population. Having not too long or too short of a life span, and the ability to reproduce in only 9 months. The human's of Monad have a few specialties that make them stand out and dominate the landscape.

All humans are able to use all simple tools, on the face of it might not sound like much but compared to the other races this gives humans easy access at no cost to all of the available everyday tools even those invented by other races. In game this takes affect from cooking tools, tools for identification of magic items, making and using mana crystals and many more.

Humans have a high bonus charisma stats compared to other races. Unlike races like the vampires where their bonus charisma only effects some races, humans effects all other races they interact with, making them strong team and political players.

Humans have well rounded daytime and night time senses, giving them a slight edge against some other races but not one sense that excels on its own.

Advantages

Advantages

Advantages: Charisma stat is very high, senses worth well in both day and night time. Sweating is a strong passive that helps in all strenuous activities. Large number of humans, will be able to live in the majority of the world. You can play a human without having to put any points into the human skill tree.
