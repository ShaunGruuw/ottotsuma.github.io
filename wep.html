Intro

Below are but a few of the many types of weapons you can discover while playing. Weapons vary like most things in this game between rarity, starting at White (Junk), Normal (blue), Rare (green), Epic (red), legendary (Orange), Unique (purple), growth-type (Seraph), Quest/inherent (gold).

As you can see in game items give [+per level], what is shown is the total stats at level 100 for all items displayed. E.g. If an item gives +40 def, it really gives +0.4 def per level.

Within each of the different ranks more enchantments and hidden enchantments are available, however not all of these will be positive additions. For instantce: Death's Oath Movement Speed: -5%, +8% resistance to death, +18 def, +30 vit, 1% life drain, You take 450 Chaos Damage per second for 3 seconds on Kill. Which has some positives and some negatives.
