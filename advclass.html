Intro

Below are but a few of the many types of classes you can use once you advance past 100. These classes are different from the smaller changes elements/jobs/spirits give, as they change the foundations of your charter.

We must briefly speak about faith. Some people will never of encountered faith before. Some classes have access to faith spells. They work in a similar way to normal spells/skills, however they do not need mana but they are limited by how much faith one has. Faith is replenished at temples and by praying, and limit useage of them. e.g. a mage may spam fire ball as long as they have the mana, but a paladin may not spam holy shield as he will be limited to something like 3 uses, before needing to pray to recover (1/3) or return to the temple to recover (3/3).

Basic Classes

Paladin

A Warrior type advanced class that has access to faith.
(Can use holy objects, cannot use unholy objects)

Pyromancer

A mage that specalises in fire magic.
(Fire spells cost 5% less)

Scout

A branch of the military rouges
(Sense + 5)

Battlemage

A Fighter or a mage that took up magic and the physical.
(Strength & Magic +1)

Theif

A Rouge that specalises in the fire finger discount.
(Dexterity + 2)
