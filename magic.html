Magic & Faith

Magic

Magic is accessable in the world by almost all Classes and Races, there are very few exceptions. The basic Mage class starts with Baisc spells unlocked, this is only true for the Mage and decendent mage classes. Basic magic starts as non-elemental spells (moving mana). This can be into objects like your weapon(Basic mana renforcement), or yourself(Mana to strength), or into a shape(Mana ball). At level 10 classes can start to attach an element to their spells like "fire" to make fire-balls. At level 30 classes can use intermidate level spells and at level 80 advanced spells.

Faith

Some people will never of encountered faith before. Some classes start with access to faith spells. They work in a similar way to normal spells/skills, however they do not need mana but are limited by how much faith one has. Faith is replenished at temples and by praying, and their useage is limited. e.g. a mage may spam fire ball as long as they have the mana, but a paladin may not spam holy shield as he will be limited to something like 3 uses, before needing to pray to recover (1/3) or return to the temple to recover (3/3).

Elements

Elements are what is mixed with mana to change the properties of the mana. Some classes can pick their elements (Mages), other classes are given elements and are unable to change them (Paladins). Elements come in a wide varity, they are sometimes catagorised into family types such as Nature, Death, Construct, etc. Below are some examples:

Nature

Fire

Fire spells come with a chance to apply a Burn DoT, they can also be used as a light source.

Nature

Water

Water spells have a chance to apply "wet" status effect, water-magic can also be used as a source of fresh water.

Nature

Lightning

Electric spells often have a Mana Drain affect & somtimes cause paralasis.

Death

Death

Pure Death magic is often used by the undead, it curses what ever it comes into contact with.

Death

Blood

Blood magic is a branch of death magic, it uses blood as an ingredient to cast spells.

Restoration field of magic arts can restore, fortify, or absorb physical and magical attributes, cure disease, and resist magical attacks
