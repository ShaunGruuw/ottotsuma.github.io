Introduction to Race

Starting Race

Your starting race is human and you can change race though in game events and quests. Your race will have stat differences to other races and sometimes unique charteristsics, but overall race makes little difference to game stats. Humans can have high Charisma when speced into, but their other stats will rely on their race. They make good commanders.

Unique Charteristsics

While each race can receive on the face of it small stat buffs, skills, abilities, etc. The real value of a race is in how it can be upgraded. Like you class you can put skill points into your race. As such if you become an Orc you might find that the race skills give more strength then your class, but doing so will not unlock class skills. So at level 30 an Orc fighter will have more strength then a Human fighter, the Human fighter is going to have access to more fighter skills. (Assuming he put all his points into fighter, and the Orc put points into "Orc".)

Basic Races

Human

Sense - +1 Charisma - +2
(Can use all simple tools and weapons)

Orc

Strength - +2 Vitality - +1
(Small health regen when in combat)

Gnoll

Dexterity - +1 Sense - +2
(Can gain access to spells/abilities early from their Demon Lord)

Miqo'te

Dexterity - +1 Sense - +2
(Their claws given them a built in weapon)

Dwarf

Strength - +1 Vitality - +2
(Small resistance to spell effects, but lower success with spells)

Rare Races

Vampire

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Magic - +1 Dexterity - +1 Sense - +1
(Blood magic is always unlocked)

Automitia

Strength - +2 Vitality - +1 Defence - +2
(Do not have stamina)

Undead (generic)

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Magic - +2 MDef - +1
(Do not have stamina)

Krogan

Strength - +2 Vitality - +2 Defence - +1
(Unable to sneak, do not suffer from fear status)

Turian

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Defence - +1 Magic - +1 Dexterity - +1
(Their scales reduce physical damage taken)

Hidden Races

Kitsune

Magic - +2 MDef - +2 Charisma - +2
(Enchanting spells are more likely to succeed)

Goblin

Vitality - +1 Dexterity - +2 Sense - +1
(Generate less agro)

Thri-kreen

Strength - +1 Dexterity - +3
(no dual wielding reduction)

Kenku

MDef - +2 Dexterity - +2
(Unknown)

Troglodytes

Vitality - +2 Defence - +1 Magic - +1
(Can produce poison with their bodies)
