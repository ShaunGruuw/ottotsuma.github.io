Intro

Your starting race is human and you can change race though in game events and quests. Your race will have stat differences to other races and sometimes unique charteristsics, but overall race makes little difference to game stats

Basic Races

Human

Sense - +1 Charisma - +2
(Can use all tools and weapons)

Orc

Strength - +2 Vitality - +1
(Small health regen when in combat)

Gnoll

Dexterity - +1 Sense - +2
(Can gain access to spells early from their Demon Lord)

Miqo'te

Dexterity - +1 Sense - +2
(Their claws given them a built in wepon)

Dwarf

Strength - +1 Vitality - +2
(Small resistance to spell effects, but lower sucess with spells)

Rare Races

Vampire

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Magic - +1 Dexterity - +1 Sense - +1
(Blood magic is always unlocked)

Automitia

Strength - +2 Vitality - +1 Defence - +2
(Do not have staminia)

Undead

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Magic - +2 MDef - +1
(Do not have staminia)

Krogan

Strength - +2 Vitality - +2 Defence - +1
(Unable to sneak, do not suffer from fear status)

Turian

Strength - +1 Vitality - +1 Defence - +1 Magic - +1 Dexterity - +1
(Their scales reduce physical dammage taken)

Hidden Races

Kitsune

Magic - +2 MDef - +2 Charisma - +2
(Enchanting is easier)

Goblin

Vitality - +1 Dexterity - +2 Sense - +1
(Generate less agro)

Thri-kreen

Strength - +1 Dexterity - +3
(no dual wielding reduction)

Kenku

MDef - +2 Dexterity - +2
(Unknown)

Troglodytes

Vitality - +2 Defence - +1 Magic - +1
(Can produce poison with their bodies)
