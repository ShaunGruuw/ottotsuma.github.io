Groups

Introduction

Groups are just groups of people (users / npcs) as the name suggests, below are some examples of groups that can be found in the world.

The basic 3

Guilds

Each Mages/Fighter/Rogues have a guild. They don`t just manage the standard 3 classes but also subclasses of thoes classes.

Faithful

Churches

These are the established faiths of each region, although they may overlap on the political map they are supported by the local goverment. Thoes religions without support are known as occults. Churches can maintain militant groups such as the templars/paladins and priests.

Enlistment

Armies

An army group can refer to a local city defence force, in particular city states and fudal goverments it also refers to a regual army maintained by a state.

Coin

Companies

These can include merchant groups, the auction houses, the arena`s, tradeing groups, mercenaries and triditional companies.

Resistance

Underworld

There are groups that exsist outside of the regular establisments, thoes such groups can include occults, bandits, assassians, nercomancers, thieves and plague doctors

Users

User made guilds

Users can form groups amongst themselves, to register as a guild they must: Acquire a Guild Petition, be at least level 30, Pick a name, an icon, aquire 3 other members and pay the local registation fee.
